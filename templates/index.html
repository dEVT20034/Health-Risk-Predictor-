About the App

HealthPredict is a research-backed AI-powered tool that helps you understand your risk for chronic diseases using parameters like glucose level, blood pressure, BMI, age, and lifestyle habits (activity, smoking). It gives you immediate insights and personalized health recommendations based on your data.
